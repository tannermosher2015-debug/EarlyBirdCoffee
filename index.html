Locally owned · Made fresh

A tiny truck with a big morning ritual.

Early Bird Coffee started with a simple idea: the best part of a Maui morning shouldn't wait in a long line.

We pull premium Vietnamese coffee, whisk ceremonial-grade matcha, and shake up milk teas — then crown every cup with cold foam infused with flavors that taste like the islands. Coconut. Ube. Black sesame. Salted caramel. Pair it with a fresh-pressed croffle or a savory panini, and breakfast practically makes itself.

No corporate playbook, no shortcuts — just a window, a great cup, and a line of regulars who know to get here early.

The Menu

Built on Vietnamese coffee, finished island-style.

A short, serious menu — every drink made to order, every foam whipped in-house. Here's what the regulars reach for.

Drinks

Coconut Cloud Vietnamese Signature

Our bold dark roast dripped over sweetened condensed milk, poured over ice, and capped with a thick coconut cold foam. The one people drive across the island for.

Ube Coconut Coffee Fan favorite

Vietnamese coffee swirled with purple-yam ube and creamy coconut — tropical, a little nutty, reviewer-rated a flat ten out of ten.

Lavender Matcha Latte

Ceremonial-grade matcha with a whisper of lavender. Smooth enough to convert the people who say they don't drink coffee.

Island Milk Teas

Silky milk teas finished with the same house cold foams. Pick your flavor, pick your foam, and we'll build it.

Cold Foams

House-whipped — add any of them to any drink.

Coconut

Toasty, tropical, and impossibly silky — the desert-island default that started it all.

Ube

Sweet purple yam with a vanilla-nutty edge. As pretty as it is addictive over dark Vietnamese coffee.

Black Sesame

Deep, roasty, and grown-up. The foam for people who like their sweetness with a little shadow.

Salted Caramel

Buttery caramel with a flake of sea salt. The crowd-pleaser that never sends anyone home unhappy.

Food

Ube Croffle

A croissant pressed into a waffle until it's shatter-crisp, then dressed with ube cream. The hidden gem hiding behind the coffee.

Savory Paninis

Pressed-to-order paninis for when coffee alone won't cut it. Warm, crisp, and exactly what a beach day needs.

Menu rotates with the seasons and what's fresh — full list & prices are posted at the truck window.
